# USACO

USACO (USA Computing olympiad) - pre-college CS & algorithmic programming competition in the US

## contest

every contest has a max score of 1000 points (typically 3 problems worth 333.33 points each)
the cutoff score to promote to the next division is around 700-800 points
if you get 1000/1000 during the 44 hour contest window, you get instant in-contest promotion and advances you to the next division (silve, gold, platinum)

time & space is just a cutoff for the test cases; not competing head to head with other contestants!

at the end of the season (US Open contest in march) top students in platinum division are invited to a summer training camp

top 4 students in this camp will be chosen to represent the US at International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI)

* anyone whos not pre-college can compete but as "observers" or "participants for fun" where you will still be placed on the scoreboard but not be selected for the US training camp nor the IOI
